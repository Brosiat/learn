Foundation

Summary vs. Analysis

Understanding the core difference is the first step to writing an excellent analysis.

Summary
Concentrates on what
Retells the content — the writer's topic, arguments, and conclusions. No comment on language or structure.
Text Analysis
Examines how
Goes one step further — it examines how the writer uses language and structure to present ideas and achieve effects on the reader.
Two key questions to ask
1
What means does the writer use?
Identify the specific technique, device, or word choice.
2
What effect / function does it have?
Explain the impact on the reader — this is the most important part.
